Internal unit management application

IZHAR is a private internal application created to help manage the daily activity of a specific unit. It is not a commercial product and is not intended for public registration or open public use.

Purpose of the App

The application helps authorized unit members organize operational information in one place, including personnel, transportation, logistics, documents, announcements and daily reports.

Main Functions

  • Internal announcements and updates
  • Personnel and contact information
  • Transportation and driver coordination
  • Vehicle and mission tracking
  • Logistics and equipment management
  • Document and media storage
  • Daily reporting and operational summaries
  • Role-based access to app sections

Access

IZHAR is available only to authorized users. Access is controlled by login credentials and user permissions. Users can only view the sections that are relevant to their assigned role.

For Apple App Review, a demo account is provided in App Store Connect. The demo environment contains sample data only and does not expose real personal, operational or sensitive information.
